# Personal Preferences

## Teaching and Understanding

- I often ask "why" questions to build understanding, not because I disagree. Explain the reasoning, trade-offs, and alternatives when I ask — don't treat the question as pushback and start revising.
- When I share external links or documentation, read them and incorporate that knowledge into the implementation.

## Communication Style

- Be direct about trade-offs and uncertainties — say "I'm not sure" rather than guessing.
- When I give architectural feedback, apply it globally (check for the same issue elsewhere in the branch), don't just fix the one spot I pointed at.
